About VULMS

The Problem We Solve

The University of Venda's traditional manual library system faced significant challenges that hindered efficient operations and user satisfaction:

  • Inefficient Tracking: Real-time tracking of book availability was difficult, leading to conflicts and student dissatisfaction.
  • Human Errors: Manual data entry in ledgers was prone to mistakes, resulting in inaccurate records of borrowed and returned books.
  • Lack of Enforcement: Challenges in consistently enforcing borrowing limits (max 3 books per user) and due dates (7-day loan period).
  • Limited Transparency: Users couldn't easily check their current borrowings or due dates, and administrators lacked clear oversight of all transactions.

Key Objectives

  • Create a comprehensive digital catalog with advanced search and filter capabilities
  • Enable seamless book borrowing and returns through an intuitive interface
  • Automatically enforce university borrowing policies (3-book limit, 7-day loan period)
  • Provide personalized dashboards for users and comprehensive admin tools for librarians
  • Deliver a responsive web application accessible on both desktop and mobile devices

Clear accountability and transparency for all library transactions.

Scalability

Robust foundation for future enhancements and features.
